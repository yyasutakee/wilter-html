Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy covers what information is collected by us when you use the Wilter website or app or contact us. In general, we collect a minimal amount of personal information - mostly for licensing.

If you do not agree with the terms of this Privacy Policy, please do not use our services. We reserve the right to modify or amend the terms of our Privacy Policy from time to time without notice.

Collection and Usage of Personal Information

FastSpring is the authorized reseller for Wilter. When you purchase a license, your name and email address are collected by FastSpring along with payment information to process your purchase and for accounting. You can review FastSpring's Privacy Policy at https://www.fastspring.com/privacy.

Names, email addresses and invoice details (but not payment method details like credit card numbers) are shared by FastSpring with us and stored on our servers. We use this information for tracking licenses and for analytics.

!!!! When you contact us over email, your name, email address and any other personal information you send to us is stored by our email host FastMail as well as our help desk service Front.

Disclosure to 3rd parties

We do not sell, rent or trade any personal information with third parties.

However, we use a number of services provided by other companies (some of which are mentioned above) for hosting, email, managing support queries, payments, analytics etc. We only share as much information with these companies as is required by them to provide us these services. All companies we we work with use industry standard practises for ensuring security and confidentiality of your information.

We may share and disclose personal information if required to do so by law or to comply with a legal process.
